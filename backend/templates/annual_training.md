# Annual Insider Risk Training

## Slide 1: Welcome
- **Title**: Protecting Our Organization Together
- **Key Message**: Everyone plays a role in security.

## Slide 2: What is Insider Risk?
- **Definition**: Risk originating from within the organization (employees, contractors).
- **Types**:
    - Malicious (Intentional theft/sabotage)
    - Negligent (Accidental data loss)
    - Compromised (Credential theft)

## Slide 3: Recognizing Indicators
- Downloading large files before resignation.
- Emailing sensitive data to personal accounts.
- Discussing confidential projects in public places.

## Slide 4: Reporting
- If you see something, say something.
- Contact: security@example.com or use the Anonymous Hotline.
